Our goal is to provide an excellent simple children's comic book based on a user prompt.

User prompt: "
$USER-PROMPT
"

Write a story with character names and details.
It must be roughly 200 words.
Use a list of 4 to 6 events that flow clearly to make the story easy to understand.
The introduction must introduce the characters and setting.
Every event must be easy to understand, very visual, and have a clear goal and cause.
Write the story such that a stupid child can understand.
The story should not be complete, the events must flow very gradually with slow pacing
